THE EASTER BUNNY IS HERE! 03/28/2020

 

The Easter event is now in effect and the Easter Bunny needs your help once again to gather eggs and fill his baskets for the childrens of Norrath!

Rogue bug fix: 03/27/2020

 

An error was found with this classe's Triple Attack skill. It was only able to be raised to 75 instead of the intented 325 cap. This has been corrected. To quickly reach the cap you can de-level to 79 then re-level to 80 and instantly earn the capped skill or you can earn the skill just by playing.

 

(Otherwise you can speak to the Grandmaster Guru to be trained.)

Tower of High Sorcery: 02/20/2020

 

Palin Majere has arrived in the Tower Lobby. #zone guildhall or #zone 345

 

We've slightly altered the way you learn the Spells and Disciplines bought for Orichalcum. You must now give the purchased Scroll or Tome to Palin and he will flag you to learn it. Before that you must visit Grandmaster Guru in the Guild lobby to train in your most up to date spells or Abilities.

 

These advanced spells are now able to be upgraded via the Stones purchased from the Orichalcum merchant when traded to Palin. Ranking up a spell will cause you to forget all the spells you have memorized and in your spell bar, so make sure you save those spells sets beforehand!

 

A few tweaks: 01/01/2020

 

PoP Progression: Updated the scripting for the Planar Projections that spawn after the Grummus and Bertox battles. They will now provide the pre and post flagging required for those zones PoP progression automatically instead of requiring you to say "flags".

 

GuildLobby: Altered the scripting for Translocator Nibiuno, he should no longer throw you into a zone loop if you don't have the required flags for the zone you are trying to reach.

 

Neighborhood: Elmindreda Farshaw has been added to the neighborhood #zone 712. She will stable any extra Dragon pets and or any Mounts you are no longer using.
