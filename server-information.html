Welcome to the Imperium EQ private server.

 

This server is mainly designed with content tuned for a Solo/Duo experience and for those who don’t have a lot of time on their hands, while still providing a challenge for players from the beginning to the end.

 

Everything going from Classic EverQuest up to the Omens of War expansion, additionally with some of The Serpent’s Spine content (while still in development), is available and fully scripted with virtually no bugs. With a new approach to progression, players will need to unlock expansions by taking down the game’s biggest foes. Custom content is also available in the end-game for experienced players to keep themselves busy for months, with more on the way! Active development is continuous, with further expansions and custom content on the horizon.

 

The End Game starts with a custom expansion developed and designed to be tackled with the help of a few other players, consider it more like a cooperation type of content instead of a solo experience.

 

Server aspect, features and main rules can be found in the box below, in-game and on the forums.

 

Currently up to:  OMENS OF WAR

Server Rules

 

This is a family friendly server, profanity in /ooc and offensive names are not welcome here. Many of our members have children that also play with them. We encourage players to keep a cool and friendly attitude in here. Any kind of toxic behavior will obviously not be tolerated.

 

MacroQuest 2 (MQ2) is not allowed to be used here, nor is needed. Any obvious game breaking macros like teleporting to a specific NPC's coordinate for example, will result in an instant ban.

 

Server Aspects

 

- Max Level: 80

 

- Account / box limit: 2 per household.

 

- Bots enabled. Create up to 20, spawn 2 at once.

 

- GM commands: #zone and #findzone are enabled to make travelling easier and  faster.

 

- Unique spells and abilites added for some classes while staying true to themselves.

 

- Unique Quests are available for special items and more.

 

- Heroes Forge Armor and weapon ornaments are available in the Guild Lobby (HUB). They will give your character a more unique  appearance.

 

- Daily Tasks available in the Guild Lobby (HUB) for daily rewards and platinum.

 

- Treasure Goblins: These are unique monsters that have a chance of spawning after killing an even con or higher foe. Killing them before they warp away will let you reap a handsome reward!

 

- Hardcore mode with automated features is turned off. This means no auto-learning or spells when leveling up. Bots are unavailable as well. If you die, your character will need to be permanently deleted.

 

- Epic 3.0’s are available and so is the Fabled Anguish!

 

- Explore the recently released zones of the Serpent’s Spine expansion with the exception of a few raids and dungeons that are still in development!

 

- Hatch, raise and breed your own Dragon pets!

 

- Tower of the High Sorcery. Face the Challenges of this 500 floors tower, the higher you go, the harder it gets!

 

- Achievement system is available. Special titles and rewards can be earned for defeating monsters and completing sets of  challenges!

 

- Reincarnate your character into a different class!

 

- An Elite drop system is available. Named mobs everywhere have a chance to drop superior +1 and +2 loot items!

 

- A gambling casino-like NPC is available in the Guild Lobby!

 

- Weapon augments, potions and poison vendors are available in the Guild Lobby to make your progression less of a hassle when needed. Make sure to use them!

 

- A Charm item quest is available at each expansion Tier NPC to make your character grow in power while pushing through content.

 

- A Stable NPC is available in the Guild Hall to store your dragon pets and mounts.

 

- A custom expansion is available for high level players looking for a tougher challenge to take on with other players.
